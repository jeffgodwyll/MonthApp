##Add,Remove and Toggle Classes
###jQuery	
	$('#box').addClass('wrap');
	$('#box').removeClass('wrap');
	$('#box').toggleClass('wrap');	
###Javascript
	var container = document.querySelector('#box');
	container.classList.add('wrap');
	container.classList.remove('wrap');
	container.classList.toggle('wrap');
